Product Definition Statement

CamLib is for individuals who are frequently creating bibliographies and/or gathering information from books, providing the ability to generate useful information about a book straight from the camera or a saved image.

User Definition

Anyone from an infrequent reader to a book enthusiast can benefit from the simple to use features of CamLib. The ideal user for CamLib is an individual relying on bibliographies and/or general information about a book. The CamLib platform is not planned to support picture books or children's books.

Market Analysis

Competing Apps and Platforms

The two main competitors for CamLib are
  1. EasyBib which provides users with a platform for creating bibliographies for a wide range of sources.
  2. Generic search engines which allow users to quickly lookup a book's information.
Ultimately, CamLib will have an edge on the competition by automatically gathering information for the user.

User and Sales Statistics

EasyBib is now used by over 34 million students and over 100 colleges, universities and high schools. A low estimate assumes that only 1% of the 34 million students, or 340,000 students, use EasyBib.com for bibliography generation. A target number of users for CamLib has been set at 1% of the estimated 340,000 students for a total of 3,400 students.

App Data Management

CamLib uses the iOS devices photo library, camera, and sharing settings (email, iMessage, AirDrop, etc.) to upload images and share generated content. Furthermore, CamLib relies on the Tesseract OCR for character recognition and the Open Library API for obtaining information.
