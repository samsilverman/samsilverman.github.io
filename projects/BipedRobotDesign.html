Biped Robot Design



I am working on the entire robot design process including kinematics, industrial design, manufacturing, electronics, simulation, and programming. The goal is to build an organic-looking biped robot using three motors per leg and manufactured with a 3D printer. Furthermore, the robot is controlled with a Raspberry Pi and incorporates a camera and IMU.

Check out a sample of images for each milestone of robot design.
